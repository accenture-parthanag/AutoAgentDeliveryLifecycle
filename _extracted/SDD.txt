1. Solution Overview

The Graphic Autonumber solution is a Microsoft Power Platform application (Power Apps canvas app + Power Automate flows + Dataverse + SharePoint) embedded as a tab in deal-specific Microsoft Teams channels. Primary actors are the Submitter (deal team member or Graphic Designer), the Graphic Designer (production FTE, 262 across Growth Markets/EMEA/Americas), and the Reviewer/Author-SME. The end-to-end journey: a deal team creates a Graphic Tracker for a Teams channel (capturing Opportunity ID, Deal Name, channel link), users add line items that are auto-stamped with a read-only Graphic Control Number in the format `<OppID>.<YY>.<Order>` (e.g., 000007.26.0001), notifications fire to the Designer on submission and 'Updates Needed', and to the Submitter/Author on 'Completed'. BT-confirmed scale: ~58,050 graphics/month with 10â€“20% FY25 growth, â‰¤300 concurrent users, and no peak spikes â€” easily absorbed by the low-code platform.

Sequence allocation strategy

On GraphicRequest create, AutonumberFlow runs synchronously and performs an atomic 'get-or-create + increment' on the OrderSequence row whose SequenceKey = <OppId>.<YY>. The increment uses Dataverse's pessimistic update with optimistic-concurrency retry (If-Match ETag): read LastOrder, compute LastOrder+1, conditional update; on 412 Precondition Failed retry up to 5x with jitter. The new order is left-padded to 4 digits and concatenated as `<OppId>.<YY>.<Order>` into GraphicRequest.GraphicControlNumber, which carries a Unique constraint as a second-line collision guard. Scope is (OpportunityId, Year) â€” not global and not per-tracker â€” so reruns and parallel designers always pull the next consecutive number for that deal-year. The field is sealed read-only after first write (BT Q2).

6. User Flows


6.1 New Graphic Request Submission & Autonumber Generation

Actor: Submitter


1. Submitter opens the Graphic Tracker tab in the deal Teams channel â€” GraphicTrackerCanvasApp loads with 'Hello <username>'.


2. Submitter clicks 'Add/Update requests in existing Graphics Tracker' and lands on the view-tracker screen for the deal.


3. Submitter clicks '+ New Item' â†’ new-item screen renders. Submitted By auto-fills from IdentityProvider.


4. Submitter fills Title, Graphics Type, Instructions, Section, Author/SME, Designer, leaves 'Email status updates to Author' = ON, and clicks Save.


5. GraphicTrackerCanvasApp writes a GraphicRequest row to DataverseGraphicStore with Status='New' and GraphicControlNumber=blank.


6. AutonumberFlow fires on row-create, atomically increments OrderSequence for key '<OppId>.<YY>', formats '<OppId>.<YY>.<NNNN>' and updates the GraphicRequest row.


7. AutonumberFlow then calls NotificationService to send the 'New Graphics for Proposal' email to the Graphic Designer.


8. GraphicTrackerCanvasApp auto-refreshes; the new line item appears in view-tracker with the read-only Graphic Control Number populated.
